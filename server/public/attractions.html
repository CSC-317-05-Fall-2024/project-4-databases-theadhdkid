Bangkok

Top Attractions in Bangkok

Wat Arun

Also known as the Temple of Dawn, featuring stunning riverside views and a majestic spire adorned with intricate porcelain tiles. The temple’s towering structure offers breathtaking views of the Chao Phraya River.

Grand Palace

The Grand Palace is a historic royal residence known for its ornate architecture and the revered Emerald Buddha. It’s a must-see symbol of Thai heritage.

Floating Market

Bangkok's floating markets feature vendors selling goods from boats along canals. It's a lively spot for fresh produce and authentic Thai street food.
